TickTask

Consistency Made Simple.

info About TickTask

TickTask is a minimalist, browser-based habit tracker designed to help you build consistency without the clutter. Unlike complex apps that require accounts and internet connections, TickTask lives entirely in your browser's local storage.

Detailed Features

  • Daily Grid: A visual calendar view to track daily habits. Lock months to preserve history exactly as it was.
  • Smart Dashboard: Visualize progress with line charts, bar graphs, and pie charts. Compare performance across different months.
  • Total Privacy: Your data never leaves your device.
  • Portable: Export your data to JSON and take it with you to any device.

How to Use

  • Start: Add habits in the Daily Grid.
  • Track: Click the boxes daily. Green means done!
  • Lock: At the end of the month, click "Done with this month?" to save a permanent snapshot of your progress.
  • Analyze: Check the Dashboard to see your streaks and consistency growing.
